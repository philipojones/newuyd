Our Story

Empowering Youth, Transforming Communities

United Youth Developers (UYD) was founded with a vision to unlock the potential of young people across Tanzania. We believe that youth are not just future leaders but present-day change-makers who can drive sustainable development in their communities.

2015

UYD was established by a group of passionate youth leaders determined to address youth unemployment and skills gaps in Tanzania.

2017

Launched our first major agribusiness program, empowering 200 young farmers with modern agricultural techniques.

2020

Expanded our programs to include digital skills training, environmental conservation, and leadership development across 5 regions.

2023

Reached a milestone of 5,000 youth directly impacted through our various programs and initiatives.

Our Mission

To empower Tanzanian youth with skills, knowledge, and opportunities for sustainable development through education, entrepreneurship, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A Tanzania where every young person has the opportunity to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Values

Youth Empowerment

We believe in the inherent potential of every young person and work to unlock their capabilities.

Community Focus

Our programs are designed with and for the communities we serve, ensuring relevance and sustainability.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions and new approaches to address youth development challenges.

Integrity

We maintain transparency and accountability in all our operations and relationships.
